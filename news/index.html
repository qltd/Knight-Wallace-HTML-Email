Wallace House at the University of Michigan is home to the Knight-Wallace Fellowships and the Livingston Awards. The two programs recognize exceptional journalists for their work, leadership and potential. Located in Ann Arbor, Michigan, on the University campus, Wallace House is a place for community, sharing knowledge, gaining inspiration and fostering excellence in journalism.
